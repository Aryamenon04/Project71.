    What's New

    • New Catering and Canteen Layout

      The new canteen shows the start of a new era. After Covid-19 hit, the school had a bit of a hard time starting back up again. However, a year back into normal school life, Woodford couldn't be better. The new and improved catering offers a wider range of food. You know have main meals and hot meals, as well as a variety of cold sandwiches. There are much more healthier drinks, with only a select few fruity fizzy drinks. There are also some sweet treats, such as cakes and brownies along with some fruits, such as apples and bananas.And all of this is just lunch. Break time has a variety of options and the children enjoy the food very much. There's lots of food, from noodles to chicken and chips in a cup and even nachos and waffles! Its a much healthier and fun place to be in. Rather than only being one or type pupils in the canteen, there's a swarm of them. You have to fight to get the food! Luckily, they always make extra now, so everyone can have a bit.

    • The Centenary Centre

      Centenary Centre was created as a tribute for the 100 years Woodford has been open. It was celebrated back in 2019. The Centenary block became a place for Maths, Physics, Biology and Chemistry to be taught. There are four floors(The lower ground floor, the ground floor, the first floor and the second floor.) Other subjects can be taught there, such as computer science and electronics. Occasionally, when teachers need to use computer rooms and there are none available, they will take us to the Centenary Centre! It is also very beautifully desgined, with there being a modern look to the inside and outside of it. For Science, there were also many labs built on the floors of the building. Students can enjoy doing experiments. I've done many; one of my favourites was doing the squeaky pop test in Year 8, as the solution made a big pop sound when it came into contact with a flame that we put inside. As well as the numerous classrooms, there are also many displays and pictures that can be found around the building. For example, on the ground floor there is a big painting of outerspace on the wall opposite some of the labs, with many facts surrounding it.I enjoy looking at it and thinking about outer space. Overall, it is a nice addition to the school.
